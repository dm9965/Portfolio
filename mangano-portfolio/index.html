GenCyber 2023 Social Media Hacking Project (Individual Project)

Designed and developed for the 2023 GenCyber Summer Camp that gives campers an interactive look into the importance of developing high quality passwords, as well as safe social media practices and reduced personal information in user posts in the form of a social media platform. Campers are given login/signup functionality, and are greeted with a feed of user posts along with a window containing usernames and input boxes where they are able to brute force hack their way into the user posts based on information they shared in their posts. The campers had so much fun with this activity the first day, they as that we created more users/posts and play the game again the next day.
